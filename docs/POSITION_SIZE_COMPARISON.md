## ⚠️ Risk Analysis at 30% Position Sizing

### Max Drawdown Scenario

**Worst case (all 3 positions hit stop loss same day):**
- 3 positions × $30,000 × 2% stop loss = **$1,800 loss**
- Still under 5% daily loss limit ($5,000)
- Circuit breaker would pause after 3 consecutive days

**Realistic risk:**
- Strategies are uncorrelated
- Unlikely all 3 stop out same day
- Expected max daily loss: $600-1,200

### Portfolio Concentration

**At 30% sizing:**
- Max positions: 3 active (3 × 30% = 90%)
- Cash buffer: $10,000 (10%)
- Diversification: AAPL + SPY + multiple strategies

**Safe enough:**
✅ Not all-in (keeps 10% cash)
✅ Stop losses limit downside
✅ Circuit breaker prevents spirals
